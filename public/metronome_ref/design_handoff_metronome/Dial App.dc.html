Dial, developed — the full app

The rotary Dial direction, carried through every screen. One tactile vocabulary — recessed wells, raised keys with hard-edge shadows — and one accent system: teal = active, amber = downbeat & transport. Everything is live — drag the dial, press play (audio on), tap the beat map to cycle accent → mute → normal, switch sounds, set count-in, and watch bars count up. The bottom tabs navigate the same instrument.
